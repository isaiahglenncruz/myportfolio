Reflexive PacMan

With a team of 2 other members at the University of California, Santa Cruz, tailored a unit of artifical intelligence to perform well in a variant game of PacMan. Teams of two PacMen battled to defend food pellets on their territory while consuming those of the enemy team. Understood the problem in terms of a Stochastic Search and optimized our PacMen's behaviors through calculated heuristics.
